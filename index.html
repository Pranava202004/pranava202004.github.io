About

Who I am & what I do

I'm Pranav, an engineer focused on embedded systems, electronics, and IoT. I enjoy building systems that combine microcontrollers, sensors, wireless communication, and real hardware to solve practical problems.

My experience includes working with STM32, ESP32, Arduino, Raspberry Pi Zero, and APM/Pixhawk-class flight controllers. I've built projects ranging from a LoRa-based weather hub to autonomous rover/drone concepts and custom home automation.

I'm looking for roles in embedded development, electronics, IoT, or VLSI (entry-level / junior), where I can work on real products, learn from a strong team, and keep pushing my hardware and firmware skills.
